Prompt Engineering & Critical Thinking in the AI Era

In the age of AI, knowing how to ask questions is as important as knowing how to solve problems. Tools like ChatGPT, Claude, and Copilot help us work faster, but using them without critical thinking can lead to mistakes.   Just like the quality of your question determines the quality of the answer, an advanced prompt doesn’t just look for solutions, it also:  

Explores alternatives

Basic Structure of a Critical Prompt

"Provide [number] alternative solutions to [technical problem]. List the tradeoffs of each and cite [official sources] if possible

Example

"Give me 3 ways to fix a React error. Explain the pros and cons of each. Include links to React’s official docs."

AI is a powerful tool, but you must ask smart questions to get good answers. Always:
-Compare different solutions
-Check official sources
-Test the code yourself
